Frequently Asked Questions

Visiting the dentist provides you with a solid foundation for lifelong oral health. These visits can prevent many common dental problems and keep your natural teeth healthy. Routine cleanings and exams are vital to fighting tooth decay, gum disease, and other issues.

Your oral hygiene routine is incredibly important. You should brush twice each day using fluoride toothpaste and floss at least once. Don’t hesitate to reach out about any symptoms or concerns you have.

You should find a dental practice that can meet all of your needs, whether preventive, cosmetic, or restorative. Also, look for a practice that has an experienced team with the skills and knowledge to provide quality care.

During your first appointment, we will have you complete some paperwork, including your health history. You can fill out a new patient form in advance to streamline the process. Our new patient form is found on the Patient Information page. We will take digital x-rays, perform a complete oral health exam and a comprehensive teeth cleaning. We will listen carefully to your concerns and make treatment recommendations based on your current oral health and your smile goals.

Yes, we work in-network with most insurance providers. We also offer a range of financing options to provide care for all patients.

Invisalign is an excellent orthodontic treatment option that can straighten your smile. Through a series of clear, barely visible aligners, Invisalign can improve appearance, function, and long-term oral health.

We recommend seeing the dentist every six months for a routine cleaning and exam, but don’t hesitate to schedule an appointment for issues that arise at other times.
